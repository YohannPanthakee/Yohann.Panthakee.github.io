Welcome

Hello! My name is Yohann Panthakee, and I am an aspiring motorsports engineer with a passion for Data Analysis & Artificial Intelligence. Welcome to my online portfolio. The purpose of this site is for me to share my work with others and keep people up to date with my projects. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing my work with you!
