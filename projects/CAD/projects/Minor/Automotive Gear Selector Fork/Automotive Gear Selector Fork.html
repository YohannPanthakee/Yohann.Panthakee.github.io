Brushless DC Motor

This is a model of a gear selector fork used in manual transmission systems

Project Aim:

To model a reletivly complex automtive compoenent based of only 2D images. These images were obtained during my automotive engineering degree at coventry University.

Software:

  • CATIA V5

Reasearch

When modelling automotive components, it's difficult to find accurately dimensioned technical drawings. My research focused on finding different views of the shifter folk to get a good understanding of the geometric features rather than getting physical dimensions
